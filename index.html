70% train, 15% validation, 15% test - split by vendor to avoid leakage
B
CNN Training
Use transfer learning (ResNet-18) with data augmentation
C

Pre-trained word vectors + character CNN, early stopping on F1 score
D
Other Models
MLP with balanced sampling, Autoencoder on normal invoices, LSTM with windowed sequences
